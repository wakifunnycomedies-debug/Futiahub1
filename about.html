About FUTIAHub

FUTIAHub is the premier digital community exclusively for the Federal University of Technology, Ikot Abasi.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between students across different departments, providing a unified space for sharing campus updates, academic resources, and a thriving student marketplace.

Why FUTIAHub?

  • Campus Updates: Real-time feed of university happenings.
  • Student Market: A safe place to buy and sell gear.
  • Privacy: Built by students, for students, with data security in mind.
